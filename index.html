About Us 关于我们

Games are our way
of creating happiness

游戏，是我们
创造快乐的方式

At DarkCat Games, we believe gaming is a hobby that brings people joy. Whether you are a pro or a beginner, whatever type of games you play — as long as you enjoy the moment, that is what matters most.

在 DarkCat Games，我们相信游戏是一种让人快乐的爱好。无论你是高手还是新手，无论你玩什么类型的游戏，只要你享受其中的时光，那就是最重要的。

We are committed to bringing fun and interactive gaming experiences to players worldwide, connecting people of all ages, cultures, and backgrounds.

我们致力于为全球玩家带来有趣、有互动性的游戏体验，连接不同年龄、文化和背景的人们。

What We Believe In

我们的理念

Three core values that guide us to create better games

三个核心价值观，指引我们创造更好的游戏

🎯

Unique & Innovative

独特创新

We reject copying. Every game pursues uniqueness in gameplay or art style. Innovation is in our DNA, and it is why players choose us.

我们拒绝抄袭，每一款游戏都追求玩法或美术上的独特性。创新是我们的 DNA，也是玩家选择我们的理由。

🌍

Global Vision

全球视野

Our games are for players around the world, transcending language and cultural barriers. We want every corner of the world to enjoy our creations.

我们的游戏面向全球玩家，跨越语言和文化的障碍。让世界每一个角落的玩家都能享受我们的作品。

❤️

Players First

玩家至上

We listen to our players, continuously optimize experiences, and make every game better and better. Player happiness is our greatest achievement.

倾听玩家的声音，持续优化体验，让每一款游戏都越来越好。玩家的快乐，是我们最大的成就。
